About Me

Hello! I'm Veronica.


I love building software projects that make life easier or solve real-world problems. I work with AI tools, large language models, and APIs, and have strong experience in web development.

I am a recent graduate from Carleton University with a Bachelor of Information Technology, specializing in Interactive Multimedia and Design. I am passionate about creating engaging user experiences and have worked on projects that combine my interests in technology, art, and design.

Outside of work, I like creating 2D/3D art and find myself getting lost in time when thinking about the next little detail to add. I also enjoy designing and developing games, and have participated in a few game jams!
