About Me

Hello! I'm Veronica.


I'm an enthusiastic, and passionate game designer and illustrator.

A lot of my experience is related to the design-process and I think the most rewarding part of being a designer is to design with others! I especially love participating in game jams and hackathons; being in a team-dynamic is where I can work the best.

Ultimately, I have an affinity towards creative spaces and when I'm not designing new ad campaigns, character designs and concept art, I enjoy playing Ultimate Frisbee and spike ball.
